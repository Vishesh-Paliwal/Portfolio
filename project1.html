Image Editor Project

An Interactive Java-Based Image Editor

Overview

Your image editor project is a powerful and user-friendly Java-based application designed to manipulate and enhance images. With a wide range of features, it allows users to:

  • Convert images to grayscale
  • Flip images horizontally and vertically
  • Invert images
  • Rotate images by 90 degrees
  • Saturate images for a vibrant look
  • Apply a blur effect for artistic appeal
  • Rotate images 90 degrees counterclockwise
  • Adjust image brightness
  • View the original image

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: The intuitive user interface makes it easy for users to load, edit, and save images.
  • Real-Time Preview: Users can see the effects of their edits in real-time, enhancing their editing experience.
  • Diverse Editing Options: A variety of editing options provide users with creative control over their images.
  • Image Export: Users can save their edited images in JPEG format for sharing and further use.
  • Brightness Adjustment: Fine-tune the brightness of images to achieve the desired visual effect.
